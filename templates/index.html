About AI Counsellor

Our AI-powered therapy platform helps users analyze their emotions and seek mental health guidance through real-time sentiment analysis and emotion detection.

AI-powered personalized therapy with real-time emotion detection is an advanced mental health support system that uses artificial intelligence to analyze emotions and deliver customized therapeutic responses in real time, enhancing emotional well-being.

Register Page

  • Users register with a username and password.
  • Credentials are securely stored in an SQLite3 database.
  • After registration, users are redirected to the login page.

Login Page

  • Users log in using their registered credentials.
  • Upon successful login, they are redirected to the Dashboard.

Dashboard Page

The Dashboard consists of three sections:

  • 📝 Text Input Field: Users answer therapy-related MCQs.
  • 📷 Web Cam Field: Captures emotions while answering MCQs.
  • 🎤 Voice Emotion Detection: Users read their responses aloud.

🔹 Key Guidelines

  • ✅ Honest answers lead to accurate analysis.
  • ✅ Ensure no distractions for precise emotion tracking.
  • ✅ AI-powered reports provide personalized guidance.
